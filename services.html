Practice Areas

The office focuses on family law and criminal defense. Within these areas, we handle a wide variety of cases in Lake County and surrounding communities.

Family Law

Family law matters often involve children, income, and long term planning for families. Whenever possible, our goal is to resolve cases through settlement while remaining prepared to go to trial when that is in the client interest.

  • Divorce and legal separation
  • Parentage
  • Child custody and visitation
  • Child and spousal support
  • Asset division
  • Domestic violence and civil harassment restraining orders
  • Guardianships

Criminal Defense

When your liberty or record is at stake, choosing the right attorney is one of the most important decisions you can make. We are dedicated to protecting our clients rights and making sure they receive fair treatment under the law.

  • Felony and misdemeanor charges
  • DUI and related offenses
  • Juvenile matters
  • Drug offenses
  • Representation from arraignment through trial

How we approach cases

Steven believes in clear communication with clients and practical advice grounded in the facts of each case. He discusses strategy with clients, explains the likely range of outcomes, and prepares thoroughly for hearings and trial.

Next steps

If you have a pending case or expect to be involved in a family law or criminal matter, contact the office to schedule a consultation. You will have an opportunity to describe your situation, ask questions, and discuss possible options.
